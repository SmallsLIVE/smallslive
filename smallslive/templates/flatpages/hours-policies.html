Hours Policies

Hours

Doors open at 7PM. Tickets are “first come/first served”.
We recommend arriving a minimum of a ½ hour before the show you would like to see to insure your seat.
Smalls sells 70 seats per show. After we reach our capacity we begin to sell tickets to the following show.
A line for ticket holders will begin 20 minutes before the show begins.

Show times are at 7:30 PM, 9:00 PM, 10:30 PM, 12:00 AM.

Smalls has an after-hours show at 1:00 AM to close.
Mezzrow has a 4:30 PM matinee show every Sunday afternoon.


Polices

• Smalls and Mezzrow have an “invitation only” booking policy. Please do not solicit us for bookings.
• There is a one drink minimum per person per show.
• Valid ID is required to purchase alcohol
• Smalls and Mezzrow reserves the right to refuse entrance or bar service at the discretion of the management.
• No smoking of any kind allowed inside the venues.
• Please be conscious of our neighbors and neighborhood and everyone’s right to abide in peace.
