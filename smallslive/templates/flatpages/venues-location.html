Reserve seats for the main shows are $40 Monday-Thursday and $50 Friday-Sunday. To guarantee your seat, please click on the show you want to see (located on the homepage and Calendar) then click on the ticket button to purchase. After purchasing your ticket, you will be sent an email with a confirmation number. Your name will be at the door at the time of the show. Please arrive 30 minutes before the show you have selected. Late comers will be seated at the discretion of the management and may lose their seats.

You may also walk-in if there’s availability – all walk-in tickets are $25.

Student prices with valid ID is $10 at the discretion of the management (mostly weekdays and late shows).

Angels, Wizards and Holy People are always free.
